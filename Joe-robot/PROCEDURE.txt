Function/Variable Details:
encoderBoundedPosition(): returns the encoder value of the NEO and returns it as either -180 or 180

Details the procedure for zeroing the motors...
STEP 1:
Hold button number 7 down on the joystick.
STEP 2:
Make all of the wheels parallel to each other and assume its zeros
STEP 3:
While still holding button 7, press button 6.
STEP 4:
Release button 7, if procedure done correctly the wheels shouldn't move when it is released.
